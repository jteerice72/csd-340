All about Olivia Desjardins

A passionate Medieval Art History student.

Can you guess what this art depicts?

Olivia's love of medieval art extends beyond merely appreciating the pieces' exquisite aesthetics. She is always analyzing the layers of symbolism, cultural context, and spiritual value that these historical works express in an effort to uncover the deeper meanings contained inside each piece. Olivia views every work as a window into the thoughts and emotions of the people who lived at that time, whether it is through the elaborate religious imagery or the portrayals of typical medieval life. Her quest to unearth these obscure stories motivates her research.
